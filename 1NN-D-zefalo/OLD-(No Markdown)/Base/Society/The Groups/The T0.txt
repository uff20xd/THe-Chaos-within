A group of the 6 strongest people being places 2-7 on the Ordinant list. The group was formed to achieve peace by forbidding fights between members of the T0. They mostly work together watching over the governments actions and govern the usage of chaos to prevent the destruction of earth and reality (mostly for egotistical reasons). Its leader is Zeero.
